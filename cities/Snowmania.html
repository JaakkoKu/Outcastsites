Snowmania quests


Dark star set quest


Recommended level: 600


From Snowmania ship, go in the middle mountain and head down. Find ladders leading down and head West past the trolls. As you find another ladders down, go first ladders right next to the last ladders. After teleport you’ll find some adonis and king yetiroths. Go past the experience door (Not the teleport before that!) and go into the teleport.

You’ll come to a place where you need to open the levers in order to continue. First, go South-West hole (Light turquoise circle) with holy knight and pull the lever. Then head North (Yellow circle) from there stairs up and follow the path to underground. Everytime you go to 1 of these 3 teleports, you get teleported to a small area with a lever you need to pull. All of the levers has to face >, if someone has been there before you but the stones are in the way, you have to pull them twice. After pulling all the levers, head to the middle (Black circle).



In the next stage you first have to pull the lever (this will reset the stones in the last area) and go down in a pitfall. From there, go East and go ladders up a couple of times and find the teleport. Head South-West corner and go into the teleport. Follow the path as shown. Last place with xarptor is a teleport hidden under a magic wall. Go there and claim your rewards.




Dark warrior set upgrade


Recommended level: 600


Requirements 1 of each:


Helmets:Full helmet of the ancients, golden helmet, demon helmet, dragonscale helmet


Armors: magic plate armor, demon armor, spider chainmail, somber armor


Legs: Valorian legs, golden legs, dragonscale legs, demon legs


Boots: Soft boots, golden boots, steel boots, somber boots


From city on Snowmania head same as Dark star set, but head North as you go ladders down from the totem pole. Follow the path as far as you find a teleport that sends you to a snow area. Follow the path to the end, you’ll face at the end some surphyres, osiris, hybrids and many other monsters. At the end you have to sacrifice all the items listed above to claim your reward.
