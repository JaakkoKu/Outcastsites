Electric talismans for Flash outfit

There are total of 6 electric talismans you need to touch around the map.


Forbidden forest

Go to Forbidden forest by boat and head East past the bridge and go North-East corner and find ladders. Go full up and then down to find the talisman at flower garden.




Tarid

Go to the grave yard East side of Tarid, same way as Deathlands and other quests.



Fallen gods

Head to fallen gods from either ship or Sir Mystic Rick and go to Fallen Gods Magicians Creek. Open the bamboo doors to enter the area with talisman.




Wrecked ship

Head to fallen gods same way as the last talisman but now go to wrecked ship area. Open the lever and go underground. The talisman is before you get to the bottom.




Beluna

From Beluna checkpoint head East stairs down, then full East and go to place with Warlocks and Surphyres. You'll find talisman at North.




Plains of gold

Follow this guide to find your way to Dark overlord set upgrade area, but don't go all the way down. The Talisman can be found -2 from main continent at East side.
