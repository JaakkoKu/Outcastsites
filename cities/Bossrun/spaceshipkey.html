Spaceship key quest, Way to Kingdom farmlands and Yalahar Abysses of Achernar

Start the quest at edron boss run 2. Go South-West teleport and you’ll find yourself with a xarptor. Here’s alot levers but all of them all fake ones so don’t pay attention to them. Just follow the road and hop into the teleport when you find one.
